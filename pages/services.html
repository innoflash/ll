PRAYER OF SALVATION

  • Your total surrender to Jesus Christ is the wisest choice you would ever have made in your life. The guarantees for a true purpose-lived life are embedded in His finished work on the Cross of Calvary. He died for all humanity to offer grace for your redeemed life of righteousness in Him. However, the choice to be a a partaker of this divine grace of salvation in Christ Jesus and the enjoyment of the fruits of the Cross depends on you. If you will let Him into your life, He stands at the door of your heart right now knocking. If you will let Him in, pray this prayer:

    Lord Jesus, Thank you for demonstrating your love for me through your painful death on the Cross of Calvary. I acknowledge my sins and thank you for shedding your blood that has washed all my sins away. I realize that I am now a new creature in you, filled with the promised Holy Spirit

    I believe you resurrected from the dead and are alive forevermore. Thank you for giving me eternal life and creating in me the power to do all things and live a prosperous life to your glory. I have peace and joy in you as long as i live for you. Thank you , dear Lord Jesus.
    Amen
