EMMANUEL MAKANDIWA

  • Emmanuel Makandiwa, founder of United Family International Ministries, and his lovely wife Ruth Makandiwa have travelled the rough and tumble of life into full ministry maturity and global prominence. The towering figure of Emmanuel Makandiwa resonates in spiritual dimensions with his stupendous impact, which compel global attention. Their thriving prophetic ministry and social influence attract awe and a never-ending thirst for God in their home country, Zimbabwe and beyond.

    Notable miracles, signs and wonders are everyday occurrences at their meetings. United Family International Church (UFIC) has over 70,000 adult and youth attendees every week.

    The ministry owns a free-to-air television channel called "ChristTV" which is aired in most parts of Africa.

    Their ministry takes care of nearly 2,000 orphans, 300-plus widows and they constantly donate to people in need.
